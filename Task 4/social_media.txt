Social Media Do’s
Use a clear and appropriate profile picture
Keep your language polite and respectful in comments
Credit original creators when sharing content
Regularly review and clean your old posts
Use social media for learning and networking
Follow relevant pages/accounts related to your field
Be mindful of your digital footprint
Report suspicious or harmful content
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
Balance your time and avoid excessive usage

Social Media Don’ts
Don’t use inappropriate profile pictures or usernames
Avoid posting when you are angry or emotional
Don’t copy or plagiarize content without permission
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or ads
Don’t accept requests from unknown people blindly
Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details
Don’t spread negativity, hate, or misinformation
Avoid posting during important academic/professional times
Don’t ignore privacy settings
Avoid addiction—don’t let social media affect your productivity
